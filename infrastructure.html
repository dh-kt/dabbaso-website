Dabbaso Town and Its Current Infrastructures

Current Infrastructure

Dabbaso is equipped with modern facilities that serve the needs of its residents. Below is a list of key infrastructure in the town.

High School

The Dabbaso High School provides quality education to over 500 students. It features modern classrooms, a library, and sports facilities.

Police Station

The Dabbaso Police Station ensures the safety and security of the town's residents. It is staffed by dedicated officers who serve the community.

Polyclinic

The Dabbaso Polyclinic offers healthcare services to the community, including general consultations, maternal care, and emergency services.

Private Clinics

Several private clinics in Dabbaso provide specialized healthcare services, including dental care, pediatrics, and diagnostics.

Market

The Dabbaso Market is a hub for trade and commerce, offering fresh produce, household goods, and handmade crafts.

Community Center

The Dabbaso Community Center hosts events, meetings, and cultural activities, fostering a sense of unity among residents.
