About The Beautiful Town of Dabbaso

About Dabbaso Town

Dabbaso, located in the Sayo-Nole district of the Oromia state in Ethiopia, has witnessed tremendous growth and transformation over the past 40 years. Once a tiny village with limited infrastructure, Dabbaso has blossomed into a bustling town.

Infrastructure

Today, Dabbaso is equipped with various infrastructures, including:

  • High School
  • Police Station
  • Polyclinic
  • Several Private Clinics
  • Various other infrastructures
